## 1) Company Overview

### Q: What is Art Craft?
A: Art Craft is an online studio shop for handmade supplies, DIY kits, and beginner-friendly craft workshops. We sell beads, yarns, paints, canvases, tools, and gift-ready project boxes.

### Q: Where is Art Craft based?
A: We are based in Helsinki, Finland. We ship across the EU and selected non-EU countries.

### Q: What are support hours?
A:
- Shop website: 24/7
- Live chat & email: Monday–Friday, 10:00–18:00 EET
- Workshop bookings: Replies within 1 business day

### Q: How can I contact Art Craft?
A:
- Email: hello@artcraft.example
- Chat: Use the help widget on our website
- Workshop desk: workshops@artcraft.example

## 2) Orders & Shipping

### Q: How long does shipping take?
A:
- Finland: 2–4 business days (standard)
- EU: 4–8 business days (standard)
- Express EU: 2–3 business days where available

### Q: Do you ship fragile items (glass, ceramics)?
A: Yes. Fragile items use extra padding and “Handle with care” labels. If damage occurs in transit, contact us within 48 hours with photos.

### Q: Can I cancel or change an order?
A: Changes are possible within 2 hours if the order has not been packed. After that, contact support—we will try to help before dispatch.

### Q: Is free shipping available?
A: Free standard shipping in Finland on orders over €59. EU thresholds vary by country (shown at checkout).

---

## 3) Returns & Refunds

### Q: What is the return window?
A: Unused items in original packaging may be returned within 14 days of delivery (EU consumer rules).

### Q: Which items cannot be returned?
A: Opened paint sets, cut fabric/by-the-meter goods, personalized engraved items, digital workshop access codes, and completed custom commissions.

### Q: How are refunds processed?
A: After we receive and inspect the return, refunds go to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

---

## 4) Workshops & Classes

### Q: What workshops do you offer?
A: Beginner sessions in watercolor, polymer clay jewelry, candle making, and seasonal wreath building. Schedules are on **Workshops** in the main menu.

### Q: Can I reschedule a workshop?
A: Yes, once per booking if you notify us at least 48 hours before the start time. Late cancellations may forfeit the seat fee.

### Q: Are materials included?
A: In-studio workshops include core materials. Online kits ship separately; the product page lists what is in the box.

### Q: Is there an age minimum?
A: Most workshops are 12+. Knife/tool sessions require 16+ or adult accompaniment for younger participants.

---

## 5) Products & Custom Orders

### Q: Do you sell wholesale or bulk?
A: Yes, for schools and small businesses. Email wholesale@artcraft.example with quantities and SKU list for a quote.

### Q: Can I request a custom gift box?
A: Yes. Custom boxes need 5–7 business days lead time. Include occasion, budget, and any allergies in the order notes.

### Q: Are your beads and paints non-toxic?
A: Products marked **CE compliant** or **AP certified** on the label meet EU safety standards for intended use. Always supervise children.

### Q: Loyalty program?
A: **Craft Club** members earn 1 point per €1 spent. Points unlock discounts and early access to workshop seats.

## 6) Payments & Technical Help

### Q: Which payment methods are accepted?
A: Card (Visa, Mastercard), PayPal, MobilePay (Finland), and bank transfer for large wholesale orders.

### Q: My payment failed—what should I do?
A: Check card limits, billing address match, and try another method. If it still fails, email support with the error message screenshot.

### Q: The chat bot could not answer my question.
A: Email hello@artcraft.example with your order number. A human agent will reply within one business day.

## 7) Policies (short)

### Q: Privacy
A: We use your data only for orders, workshops, and support. We do not sell personal data. See our Privacy Policy on the website footer.

### Q: Gift cards
A: Digital gift cards are valid 24 months from purchase. Non-refundable except where required by law.
