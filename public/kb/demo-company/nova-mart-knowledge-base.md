# NovaMart E-Commerce Knowledge Base & Customer Support Playbook (v1.0)

**Last Updated:** May 5, 2026  
**Owner:** Customer Support Operations  
**Applies To:** Website, Mobile Web, and Customer Support Channels

---

## 1) Company Overview

### Q: What is NovaMart?
A: NovaMart is an online retail company offering electronics, fashion, home essentials, beauty products, and lifestyle accessories. We focus on reliable delivery, secure payments, and responsive customer support.

### Q: Where does NovaMart operate?
A: NovaMart is headquartered in Austin, Texas, USA. We sell and ship across multiple countries, including the US, Canada, UK, EU regions, and selected countries in Asia and the Middle East.

### Q: What are NovaMart's business hours?
A:
- Website/App: 24/7
- Customer Support (Live Chat & Phone): Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM-7:00 PM EST
- Email Support: 24/7 intake, first response usually within 24 hours

### Q: How can customers contact support?
A:
- Email: support@novamart.com
- Phone: +1 (800) 555-9321
- Live Chat: Available from the Help Center during support hours

## 2) Account & Profile Management

### Q: Do I need an account to place an order?
A: No. Customers can check out as guests. However, creating an account allows order tracking, address saving, faster checkout, and easier returns.

### Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
A: Click **Forgot Password** on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

### Q: Can customers update account details?
A: Yes. Go to **My Profile > Account Settings** to update name, password, phone number, and saved addresses.

### Q: Is customer information secure?
A: Yes. We use SSL encryption, secure tokenized payments, and role-based access controls internally. We do not sell customer personal data.

---

## 3) Orders & Checkout

### Q: How does a customer place an order?
A:
1. Add items to cart.
2. Review quantity/variants in cart.
3. Click **Checkout**.
4. Enter shipping details.
5. Choose payment method.
6. Confirm and place order.

### Q: Can customers modify or cancel an order?
A: Limited changes are possible within 1 hour if the order has not entered packing/shipping. Cancellations are possible before shipment.

### Q: How do customers track orders?
A: Tracking details are sent by email/SMS (if provided) and visible in **My Orders** after dispatch.

### Q: Why did I not receive order confirmation?
A: Check spam/junk folders first. If missing after 1 hour, contact support with payment timestamp and registered email.

---

## 4) Shipping & Delivery

### Q: Where does NovaMart ship?
A: We ship to major supported regions (US, Canada, UK, EU, and selected international destinations). Availability depends on product and carrier network.

### Q: What shipping options are offered?
A:
- Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
- Express Shipping: 1-3 business days
- Same-Day/Next-Day: Selected cities only

### Q: Is free shipping available?
A: Yes, free standard shipping is available on eligible orders above specified cart value in supported regions.

### Q: What if a package is delayed or lost?
A: Support opens a carrier investigation if delivery is beyond the expected window and arranges replacement/refund if eligible.

---

## 5) Returns, Exchanges & Refunds

### Q: What is the return window?
A: Most products are returnable within 30 days from delivery date.

### Q: How to initiate a return?
A:
1. Go to **Orders > Request Return**.
2. Select item and reason.
3. Download/print return label (if provided).
4. Pack item and ship/drop off.

### Q: How are refunds processed?
A: After return is received and inspected, refund is issued to original payment method within 5-7 business days.

### Q: Which items are non-returnable?
A: Final sale/clearance items, opened hygiene products, undergarments/swimwear (if opened), personalized/custom items, and gift cards.

---

## 6) Payments & Billing

### Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna/Installments (region-dependent).

### Q: Is checkout secure?
A: Yes. We use encrypted transactions and PCI-compliant payment processing.

### Q: Why was a payment declined?
A: Common reasons include bank restrictions, incorrect card details, insufficient funds, or fraud checks.

### Q: When is payment charged?
A: Payment is charged immediately after successful order confirmation.

---

## 7) Products & Inventory

### Q: Are products authentic?
A: Yes. Products are sourced from trusted manufacturers, authorized distributors, or vetted suppliers.

### Q: How can customers check stock?
A: Stock status is shown on product pages: **In Stock**, **Low Stock**, or **Out of Stock**.

### Q: Do sold-out items get restocked?
A: Many items are restocked. Customers can use **Notify Me** for restock alerts.

### Q: Do products include warranty?
A: Eligible electronics may include a 1-year limited warranty. Details are listed on product pages.

## 8) Technical Troubleshooting

### Q: The website is slow or not loading. What can I do?
A: Refresh page, clear cache/cookies, disable browser extensions/VPN, or try another browser/device/network.

### Q: I cannot log in. What should I do?
A: Reset password first. If issue continues, contact support with your email and screenshot of the error.

### Q: Tracking link is not updating.
A: Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours after shipment handoff.

### Q: Promo code is not working.
A: Check expiration date, minimum spend, category restrictions, and whether the code was already used.

## 9) Privacy, Security & Policy

### Q: How is personal data used?
A: Data is used for order processing, support, fraud prevention, and service improvement. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

### Q: Can customers request account/data deletion?
A: Yes. Customers can request deletion by contacting privacy@novamart.com from their registered email.

### Q: Do you charge sales tax?
A: Yes. Taxes are applied based on destination and local regulations at checkout.

### Q: Do you offer price matching?
A: Price matching is not generally offered unless part of an official campaign.

## 10) Human Handoff & Escalation

### Q: How can customers talk to a human agent?
A: Customers can type **"Connect me to an agent"** in chat or contact support by email/phone.

### Q: When should chatbot escalate?
A: Escalate for payment disputes, legal/privacy concerns, fraud/security concerns, repeated unresolved issues, or when explicitly requested by customer.

### Q: What details are needed before escalation?
A:
- Order number
- Registered email
- Issue summary
- Error screenshot (if available)
