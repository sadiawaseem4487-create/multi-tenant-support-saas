Customer support platform

AI help for every company—without mixing their data

One product. Many organizations. Each with its own knowledge, brand, and chat.

Live demo: multi-tenant-support-saas.vercel.app

The problem

One chatbot is not enough for SaaS

When you sell software to many companies, each customer needs answers from their policies—not someone else’s.

  • Privacy — Company A’s documents must never appear in Company B’s replies
  • Branding — Each client wants their name, colors, and tone
  • Control — Owners invite staff and update knowledge without calling engineering every time

Say in your own words “Think of it like apartment buildings: same building system, separate locked units.”

2

Our solution

What we built in one sentence

A web platform where each organization signs in, uploads knowledge, and gets a branded AI chat—powered by smart automation behind the scenes.

For business users

Admin dashboard: invite team, upload help content, see if the bot is healthy, read chat analytics.

For end customers

A simple chat on a custom web address—or a widget embedded on their website.

Say “You don’t need to understand AI models to run it—if you can use email and a website, you can use this.”

Key message “We split what users see from how answers are produced. That makes the product flexible.”

4

Star of the show

What is n8n—and why we use it?

n8n is a visual automation tool. It connects steps: “when a question arrives → find relevant text → ask the AI → send the reply.”

Without n8n

Every change to how the bot thinks would require programmers to redeploy the whole app.

With n8n

Operators can adjust workflows, swap AI providers, and add steps (e.g. email invites) in a flowchart—faster and safer for experiments.

Analogy: n8n is the kitchen; our website is the restaurant front of house. Guests never go into the kitchen, but that’s where the meal is prepared.

5

n8n · Workflow 1

When someone asks a question in chat

1. Visitor types a question on your branded chat page ↓ 2. Our website tags the question with which company it belongs to ↓ 3. n8n: find similar paragraphs in that company only ↓ 4. n8n: ask the AI to answer using those paragraphs ↓ 5. Friendly answer appears in the chat window

Why this matters: The AI is not “making things up from the whole internet”—it is guided by that organization’s approved content.

6

n8n · Workflow 2

When a company adds knowledge

1. Admin uploads a document or points to a help page ↓ 2. Our system starts a tracked “ingest job” (visible in admin) ↓ 3. n8n: read text → split into small pieces → convert to searchable “memories” ↓ 4. Memories are stored under that company’s name only ↓ 5. Admin sees “success” and how many pieces were indexed

Business value: Updating the bot is like updating a FAQ—no code release required once the pipeline is connected.

7

n8n · Workflow 3

When you invite a teammate

  • Owner enters an email and role in the admin panel
  • Our app creates a secure invitation link
  • n8n can send the welcome email automatically (optional workflow)
  • New person signs in and lands in the right organization

n8n is not only for chat—it is our general integration layer for emails and future automations (CRM, Slack, etc.).

8

Trust & safety

How we keep companies separate

Separate memory

Every piece of knowledge is labeled with the company it belongs to.

Separate search

When answering, the system only searches that company’s memories.

Locked admin

Only signed-in staff with the right role can change settings or knowledge.

Secret handshakes

Our website and n8n verify each other with a private key—random people cannot trigger workflows.

Why this approach wins

Benefits for the business

  • Faster onboarding — New client gets a URL, brand, and knowledge base in days, not months
  • Lower engineering cost — AI logic lives in adjustable workflows, not buried in app code
  • Transparency — Ingest jobs, analytics, and audit log show what happened
  • Room to grow — Billing, more languages, Slack handoff can plug into n8n later
